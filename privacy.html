Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Data Collection

AlgDrill collects minimal user data to provide cloud-syncing services. This includes your email address provided during sign-up and your speedcubing statistics (solve times, status of algorithms, and custom algorithm preferences).

Use of Information

Your data is used strictly for the following:

  • To allow you to log in and access your data across different devices.
  • To save your progress through algorithm sets.
  • To store your personal solve history.

Data Security

We use Google Firebase for authentication and database management. We do not have access to your raw passwords. Your email is stored securely in the Firebase Authentication system.

User Rights

You have the right to access the data we hold about you or request its permanent deletion from our servers. To request data removal, please contact our support email.

Third Parties

AlgDrill does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data to outside parties. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising.
